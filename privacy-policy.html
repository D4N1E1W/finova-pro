Privacy Policy

Any capitalized terms not defined herein shall have the definition provided in the Site's Terms of Service. This Policy applies to your use of this Site.

The Information FINOVA Collects

Like most websites, the Site collects certain information automatically and stores it in log files. This information is technical and usage information and may include information about how you use our website and Services (e.g., the pages you view, the links you click, etc.), information about your browser and online usage patterns (e.g., Internet Protocol address, browser type, browser language, referring / exit pages and URLs, pages viewed, whether you opened an email, links clicked, etc.), and information about the device(s) you use to access the website and Services (e.g., mobile device identifier, mobile carrier, device type, model and manufacturer, mobile device operating system brand and model, and whether you access the website and Services from multiple devices).

FINOVA collects technical and usage information to fulfil our legitimate interest in understanding how website visitors use the website, and to assist us in more effectively administering the Site and providing the Services. FINOVA uses certain cookies, and more information on these cookies and our use of them can be found on the Privacy Policy.

FINOVA may use aggregated data about our Site users and disclose the results of such aggregated (but not personally identifiable) information to our partners, service providers, advertisers, and/or other third parties for our business purposes.

1.2 Information You Submit to FINOVA Through the Site

The Site has several features through which you are able to enter personal information. When you fill out a form or provide information through the Site, FINOVA will use that information for the purpose for which it was collected and for other reasonably related purposes FINOVA determines are appropriate. FINOVA may provide your information to third parties for the processing of that information solely for FINOVA's benefit, but FINOVA will not provide your information to any third party for marketing purposes except in connection with information requests you make that involve third parties or in connection with events that involve presentations or sponsorship by third parties.

If you correspond with us by e-mail, the postal service, or other form of communication outside of the Site, we may retain such correspondence and the information contained in it and use it to respond to your inquiry; to notify you of FINOVA's presentations, publications, or other services; or to keep a record of your inquiry, request, and the like. We have a legitimate business purpose for maintaining personal information of those who communicate with FINOVA voluntarily.
